As you peer closely from Water Street onto the docks, the moon casts an eerie glow on the scene. The water, once tranquil, now ripples 
with unsettling movements beneath the wooden planks. Dimly lit lanterns reveal the silhouettes of Deep Ones, their amphibious figures 
scavenging amidst discarded nets and debris. Patrols, cloaked in shadows, weave through the structures, their eyes ever watchful.
